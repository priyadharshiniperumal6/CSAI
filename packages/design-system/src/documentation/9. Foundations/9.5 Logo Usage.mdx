## Usage Guidance

1. Prefer SVG and keep aspect ratio intact.
2. Never recolor or stretch the mark.
3. Keep clear space around the logo (at least 8px on all sides in compact surfaces).
4. For dark backgrounds, keep a high-contrast logo treatment (current white logotype is valid).
